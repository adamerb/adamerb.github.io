My history with programming...

I wrote my first "Hello World" function in my junior year of college. As an industrial engineering student, I was learning to code in Python primarily with a focus to build business model optimizations. When I finished college, I was left with a deep curiosity and thirst for more code. I hopped online to do some research and that is when I found the world of front end development. This was the most pivotal moment for me because I was able to see the power of building both client and server side software.
